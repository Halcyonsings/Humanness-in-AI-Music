About This Task
This experiment aims to study music composed by humans or artificial intelligence (AI). We hypothesize that human composers are better than AI composers, because AI has not yet surpassed humans in some areas. For example, in poem-writing, AI cannot capture the meaning of a poem as well as humans can ; in translation, machines are unable to capture the nuance that comes from cultural influence.
