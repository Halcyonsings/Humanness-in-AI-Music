Listening Session
Please read the notice before listening to the clips:

  1. Please wear headphones when listening.
  2. In this section, you will listen to 12 different clips. A questionnaire will appear below after the music being completely played.
  3. You cannot skip any music clips. Each music clip will be played once and only once. So please listen carefully.
  4. In the questionnaire, you will have to answer 27 items. Please rate each music clip according to feelings or musical features on a scale ranging from 1 (not at all) to 5 (very much).
The 5-point Scale:
1                 2                  3                  4                  5
Not at all    Somewhat    Moderately    Quite a lot    Very much
